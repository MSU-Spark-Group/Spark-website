The SPARK group has been awarded a Michigan State University Shared Research Infrastructure Program (SRIP) grant to develop a mid-infrared dual-comb spectroscopy user facility. This new shared facility will provide advanced molecular sensing capabilities for research in advanced manufacturing, energy, environmental science, and agriculture. Check out the [announcement](https://research.msu.edu/news/shared-research-infrastructure-programs-srip-january-2026-awards-announced).
