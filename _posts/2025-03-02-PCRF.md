SPARK's collaborative research (user) proposal “Understanding the Non-Equilibrium Hydrogenation Mechanism for Biodiesel Up-conversion" to the Princeton Collaborative Low Temperature Plasma Research Facility (PCRF) has been accepted and will be given a runtime on our Facility in FY25/26 (2025).
