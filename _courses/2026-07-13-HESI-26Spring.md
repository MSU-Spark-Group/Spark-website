This program is designed for rising high school sophomores, juniors, and seniors who are seriously considering engineering as a future career. The SPARK Group is honored to introduce future Spartan engineers to Mechanical Engineering. During the ME session, we also provide hands-on activities through “Utilizing Optical Emission Spectroscopy to Diagnose Gases,” allowing students to explore why and how light can interact with gases. We also showcase our latest research through a presentation entitled “Innovations Beyond Optical Emissions.”
