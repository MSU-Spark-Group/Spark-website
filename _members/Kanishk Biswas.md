Kanishk Biswas is working on a laser-engraving project that aims to develop a compact, high-precision system capable of engraving on glass. His work includes designing and optimizing the mechanical setup, integrating control electronics, and refining engraving parameters. He primarily uses the LightBurn software to create and control engraving patterns, including precision-cut circular designs on glass. His goal is to build a reliable and user-friendly engraving device that can produce clean and detailed results on transparent materials.
